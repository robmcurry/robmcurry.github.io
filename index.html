About Us

Robert M. Curry is an assistant professor of industrial engineering at the University of Arkansas, where he leads a dynamic research group focused on optimization and network flow analysis. With a passion for addressing real-world challenges, Dr. Curry's work delves into the intricate intersections of industrial systems and mathematical modeling, striving to enhance the efficiency and resilience of complex networks. His research interests encompass the development of cutting-edge algorithms for solving large-scale optimization problems, with a particular emphasis on applications in transportation logistics, supply chain management, and telecommunications. Committed to fostering innovation in the field, Dr. Curry actively collaborates with industry partners and mentors aspiring engineers, fostering a new generation of problem-solvers and thought leaders in the realm of industrial engineering.
